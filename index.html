Atrio

Atrio is a company that a couple of my friends and I started a few years ago. We create web profile videos for clients to display on their sites. I helped out on the development of our site.

Tuzola

Tuzola is a Chicago-based travel startup company. They've only been around for about a year but they're making a splash in the travel industry with their one of a kind business model: "Book now, pay later." Customers are given the option to purchase their airfare through installment payments. I was tasked with creating their Contact and About Us page.

Kolea Airways

For our Mobile Media Design 2 class, I created this mobile application for Kolea Airways, a fictional Hawaiian airline.

Swipebox Demo

In our Front End Frameworks class, one of our assignments was to choose a framework and demonstrate its functionality. I chose to learn how to use SwipeboxJs, a simple JavaScript framework that puts photos into a swipeable photo gallery.

Atrio Variation

Here's a simple variation that I came up with for Atrio's website. The focus of this project was to use the HTML canvas feature which can be seen in the downward facing arrows. This site also takes advantage of the HTML video tags.

World Surf Usability Test

World Surf is an application used by surfers around the world that provides first-hand insight on various surf destinations. My job was to design a wireframe and upload them to Usability hub for testing the interfaces.

Autism Awareness Site

Autism Speaks was another project I worked on while I was in school. I built this site based on autismspeaks.org. My goal with this project was to create a site that's fully responsive using the popular Twitter Bootstrap framework.

Vimeo Channel

My vimeo channel has a collection of all of my project presentations from school.

RaynaType

RaynaType was the last project I worked on with Atrio. Rayna is a calligrapher who's story we captured in one of our short films.
